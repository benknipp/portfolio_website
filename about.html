Hi, My name is Ben.

I am a UX Designer with a corporate background in Finance and Business Analysis.

So why UX then? Why don't you continue to crush numbers and processes?

Simple, really. I want to make a positive impact in this world by making technology accessible, easy and fun to use while it is being utilized to solve user-centered problems.

With two small kids I have a strong interest in sustainable solutions and what we leave behind for succeeding generations. I am a strong believer in minimalism, guiding almost all I do.

I love photography, travelling and meeting new people and cultures. Probably the reason I left Germany to live in Ireland, the Czech Republic and now Canada.
